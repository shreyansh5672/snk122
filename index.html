Raghuraj Pratap Singh, popularly known as Raja Bhaiya, is an Indian politician. He is an M.L.A. of Jansatta Dal in the 18th Uttar Pradesh Assembly from his native local assembly constituency Kunda. On 16 November 2018, Singh announced he is forming his own party, the Jansatta Dal Loktantrik.
